About Earners

Open badges are ideal for persons who have acquired skills through workplace experience or learning on their own. A micro-credentialing approach is used where earners can quickly achieve 'bite size' badges. The system also allows earners to learn a new skill by undertaking learning to complete tasks assigned by an assessor.

About Your Assessor

Each earner is assigned an online assessor. The assessor will provide you with a project to complete, and provide you with learning materials to improve your knowledge and skill. The assessor will review your project and provide feedback to ensure you achieve the criteria for your badge. When the assessor is satisfied with your work, you wil be recommended for a badge.

About GitHub Classroom

You will complete projects using GitHub Classroom. You assessor will review your project on GitHub and provide you with feedback.

About Portfolios

You will create an online portfolio of your work. Your portfolio will contain images of your completed projects. It will also contain the badges you have achieved. You can also put information relating to your education and experience on your portfolio.
