About Mentors

Some time you get stuck with coding and need help from a real person. We provide mentors to work with you during your learning or when you are working on acquiring your digital badges. You have the option of an online mentor or a face-to-face mentor. If a face-to-face mentor is available in your country you will get both!

Online Mentors

Online mentors will help you with your coding via screen sharing and chat on Microsoft Teams. Online mentoring is available in all countries. However, we only support the English language at this time.

Face-to-Face Mentors

You can arrange individual mentoring sessions with your mentor for a few hours every week or on weekends. You can get help in person by your mentor. Face-to-Face mentoring is only available in Trinidad at this time.
