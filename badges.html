About Badges

A micro-credentialing approach is used where learners can quickly achieve 'bite size' badges. This allows a learner to immediately put their skills to work. Our badges are based on the Open Badges V2.0 Specification published by the IMS Global Learning Consortium

View our Badges

About Your Mentor

Each learner is assigned an online mentor. The mentor will provide you with a coding project to complete. The mentor will review your project and provide feedback to ensure you achieve the criteria for your badge. When the mentor is satisfied with your work, you will be recommended for a badge.

About GitHub Classroom

You will complete coding projects using GitHub Classroom. Your mentor will invite you on GitHub to complete an assignment. The mentor will review your project in your GitHub repository and provide you with feedback online.
